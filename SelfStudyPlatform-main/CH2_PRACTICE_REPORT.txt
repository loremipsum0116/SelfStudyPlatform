Reason for exclusion
--------------------
The 2.10 series depends on signed overflow judgment, and in 2.10.5 ~ 2.10.6 it also leans on overflow/exception-aware execution semantics.
Under the current project structure, overflow is classified on the Ch.3 concept side rather than the Ch.2 side.
Keeping 2.10 inside Ch.2 practice would therefore blur the chapter boundary.
